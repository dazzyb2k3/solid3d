Over the years we have provided our expertise to projects in several sectors.  Most recently these have included:

Construction

We continue to be involved with several high profile contruction projects in the London area.  Most recently these have included the Atlas building on City Road and the ongoing improvements at the Old Bailey.


Oil & Gas

Solid3D have worked extensively in the Oil & Gas Industry providing design, CAD and Cloud Data services for extensive upgrades and retro fitting of safety equipment to rigs, platforms and vessels across the globe for companies such as Total and BP.

Automotive

Our design professionals have contributed to the work on critical components for McLaren Mecedes as well as providing assets for commercial grade racing simulations.

Aerospace

Solid3D staff have worked on system detailing for ground breaking aerospace projects at Airbus.

Education

Solid3D were used as BIM coordinators for the contruction of six schools in the South East from 2016-2018.


Residential

We have worked with many london residents to provide building models and drawings to assist with new contructions, building improvements and planning applications.

BIM Content Creation

Do you have a list of products that you want to be made into BIM objects?

Our highly experienced in-house Revit team can create a library of your manufactured products into parametric BIM objects, also known as Revit families. These BIM objects can be provided for your company’s own use or third-party BIM content websites such as bimstore or NBS National BIM Library.
